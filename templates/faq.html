  • Rape

  • What is rape?

    Rape is a type of sexual assault involving sexual intercourse or penetration without the consent of the individual.

  • How does your project help prevent rape?

    Our project utilizes facial expression recognition, AI-based predictive models, and alert systems to identify potential threats and take preventive actions. By monitoring real-time data, our system can send alerts and offer support, potentially preventing incidents before they occur.

  • What should I do if I am raped?

    It's important to get to a safe place and seek immediate medical attention. Contact authorities and consider talking to a counselor for emotional support.

  • How can I report rape?

    You can report rape by contacting the police or visiting a local hospital. They will guide you through the process of gathering evidence and making a formal report.

  • Sexual Harassment

  • What constitutes sexual harassment?

    Sexual harassment includes any unwelcome sexual advances, requests for sexual favors, and other verbal or physical harassment of a sexual nature.

  • How does your facial expression recognition work to prevent harassment?

    Our facial expression recognition technology analyzes facial cues and body language to detect signs of distress or discomfort. When potential harassment is detected, our system can alert authorities or designated contacts to intervene swiftly.

  • How do I handle sexual harassment at work?

    If you experience sexual harassment at work, document the incidents and report them to HR or a trusted supervisor. You may also seek legal advice or support from organizations that specialize in workplace harassment.

  • What are my legal rights if I'm sexually harassed?

    You have the right to work in a harassment-free environment. Legal protections are available under various laws, including the Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (EEOC) regulations.

  • Domestic Violence

  • What is domestic violence?

    Domestic violence involves abuse or violence by one person against another in a domestic setting, such as in marriage or cohabitation. It can be physical, emotional, psychological, or sexual.

  • How does your AI-based chatbot assist victims of domestic violence?

    Our AI-based chatbot offers 24/7 support to victims of domestic violence by providing information on safe shelters, legal rights, and steps to ensure personal safety. It also connects victims to human support agents when necessary.

  • How can I seek help if I’m a victim of domestic violence?

    If you're a victim of domestic violence, reach out to local shelters, hotlines, or support groups. It's crucial to create a safety plan and consider legal action such as restraining orders.

  • What are the signs of domestic violence?

    Signs include physical injuries, isolation from friends and family, financial control, emotional manipulation, and constant fear of a partner.

  • Assault

  • What should I do if I’m assaulted?

    First, ensure your safety by moving to a secure location. Then, seek medical attention and report the incident to the police. It's also advisable to get in touch with support services for further assistance.

  • How does your project use predictive models to prevent assaults?

    We utilize AI-powered predictive models that analyze behavior patterns and environmental factors to identify high-risk situations. This allows us to issue timely alerts and take preventive actions to reduce the likelihood of an assault.

  • How can I protect myself from assault?

    Being aware of your surroundings, avoiding risky situations, and learning self-defense can help protect against assault. It's also helpful to stay in well-lit, populated areas and to trust your instincts.

  • What are the legal consequences of assault?

    Assault is a criminal offense that can result in significant legal consequences for the perpetrator, including fines, imprisonment, and a criminal record.

  • Precautions

  • How can I protect myself from sexual violence?

    Stay aware of your surroundings, set clear boundaries, and avoid isolated areas. Trust your instincts and don't hesitate to leave situations where you feel unsafe.

  • How does your project keep me informed about daily news related to rape and harassment?

    Our project includes a daily news feature that keeps you updated on the latest incidents, legal changes, and awareness campaigns related to rape, harassment, and violence. This ensures you stay informed and aware of your surroundings.

  • What should I do if I witness someone being harassed or assaulted?

    If you witness harassment or assault, try to intervene safely or call for help. Being a proactive bystander can prevent further harm.

  • What safety measures should I take in public spaces?

    When in public spaces, avoid walking alone at night, stay in well-lit areas, and have your phone readily accessible. It’s also wise to inform someone of your whereabouts.

  • Safety Code

  • What is a Safety Code?

    A Safety Code is a discreet way to alert someone that you are in danger or need help without raising suspicion. You can use these codes in various situations to communicate distress or specific needs.

  • What does the code "1226" mean?

    The code "1226" means "I am in danger." Use this code to alert someone that you need immediate help.

  • What are some other Safety Codes I can use?

    Here are some additional safety codes:

    • 9117: "I need you to call the police immediately."
    • 1031: "Please check on me, I'm not feeling safe."
    • 2245: "I am with someone who is making me uncomfortable."
    • 3309: "Please stay on the phone with me, I might be in danger."
    • 4455: "I need you to contact my emergency contact."
  • How should I use a Safety Code?

    You can use a Safety Code by texting it to a trusted contact, using it in conversation, or through any means of communication where you can discreetly send the code. Make sure your trusted contacts know what the codes mean and what actions they should take when they receive one.
